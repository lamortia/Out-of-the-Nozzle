How to follow upcoming events

Upcoming booth dates and local appearances should be posted through the business Facebook page first. That keeps the website simple while giving customers one clear place to check for updates.

Event pickup

Customers can message ahead of time, place an order, and arrange pickup at a local booth event when availability is confirmed. Local pickup is always available, so event pickup acts as an extra convenience.

What customers should know

Booth inventory can vary by event, so specific items are not guaranteed unless they are arranged ahead of time. The strongest path is: browse the website, message with questions, then choose shipping, local pickup, or event pickup.
